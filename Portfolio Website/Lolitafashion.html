Lolita Fashion Screenprint

Lolita Fashion is an iconic Japanese Fashion, which
first started in Harajuku, Tokyo.

I created a series of different coloured screenprints
inspired by Lolita Fashion.

I decided to display the peach coloured screenprint
for the DGC Exhibition.The peach colour depicts the
innocence and delicate nature of the fashion
