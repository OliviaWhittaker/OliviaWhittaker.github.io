Cass Art Watercolour Challenge 2017:
Pineapple Print Design

I submitted this Pineapple Print Design to the
Cass Art Watercolour Challenge 2017.
I decided to create a simple summer, tropical
themed print design.

Firstly I sketched out the image and painted it
with watercolour paints. I then scanned in the
image and created the print formation using
Adobe Photoshop. I think that this print would
look good as a wallpaper print, fashion and
accessory print,stationary print, wrapping
paper print etc.
