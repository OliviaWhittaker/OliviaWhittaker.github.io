Cath Kidston YCN Student Awards

My print 'Bumble Bee' in the colourway 'Duck Egg
Blue' was commended by the Cath Kidston design
team and featured in the Cath Kidston Summer
Exhibition at the Cath Kidston 180 Picadilly
Flagship store. I was inspired by vintage china
honey pots that I found at home and the great
British countryside.

“It was wonderful to see all the
hand-drawn elements.
Fantastic subject matter and a beautiful illustrative hand-writing to the print.
We love the sense of humor that the
bumble-bee brings to the print.”

-Cath Kidston
